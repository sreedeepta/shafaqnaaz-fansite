Shafaq Naaz is an Indian television actress known for her impactful roles in mythological and drama series.

She rose to fame for playing Kunti in *Mahabharat* and has since continued to amaze fans with her performances in shows like *Devi Adi Parashakti*, *Chidiya Ghar*, and more.

Beyond the screen, she’s known for her profound quotes, grounded nature, and inspiring personality.
